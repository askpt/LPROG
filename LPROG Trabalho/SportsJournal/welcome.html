Welcome to the Sports Journal

What is the "SportsJournal"?

The "Sports Journal" is an academic project developed in the School of Engineering of the Polytechnic of Porto in the scope of "Languages and Programming", which is tied to the second year of the degree on Informatics Engineering. In this project we intend to show several sport news (collected from well-known references) employing basic XML and XSLT tecnhnology. If you're looking for technical information about the project itself please refer to the ABOUT menu above.

Navigating through "SportsJournal"

The menu above is self-explanatory and provides different options to the user. The menu NEWS gives access to all the news collected in the aim of this project, however you can look at them in two essential perpectives: by selecting them all or by applying a specific filter. If you choose the latter option you'll be guided to an additional page where you can apply the given filters accordingly

The export to XML option allows you to visualize a xml document where all the available information was reorganized in a different pattern when compared to the one used to display the news.
